## What rights you have

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- You can request information about how your personal data is processed
- You can request that anything inaccurate in your personal data be corrected
- You can request that your personal data be deleted
- You can request that the processing of your personal data is restricted
- You can withdraw an earlier consent to processing your data
- You can object to the processing of your data
- You can request a copy your personal data in a common, machine-readable format

If you wish to make any of these requests, please get in contact with our data protection officer.

If you're unhappy with our response, you can contact the regulating authority, the _[Hessischer Beauftragter für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit](https://datenschutz.hessen.de/)_.

## What data we collect and why

### Log files

Our website is hosted by Netlify, Inc ("Netlify"). When you visit our website, Netlify records standard data about your request, including your IP address, the time of your visit, and your browser.

We need this information to ensure the reliability and security of our website (Art. 6, GDPR). It is generally retained for 30 days.

Netlify acts as a data processor (Art. 28 GDPR ). More information is available at [www.netlify.com/trust-center](https://www.netlify.com/trust-center).
