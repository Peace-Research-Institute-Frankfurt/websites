Prof. Dr. Christopher Daase is Co-Speaker of CNTR, Professor of International Relations at Goethe University Frankfurt and Deputy Director of the Peace Research Institute Frankfurt (PRIF) where he heads the Research Department International Security. His research and teaching focuses on security policy (especially wars and civil wars, terrorism and radicalization, non-proliferation and arms control) as well as international institutions.
