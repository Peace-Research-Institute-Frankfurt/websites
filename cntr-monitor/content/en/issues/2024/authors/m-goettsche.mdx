Prof. Dr. Malte Göttsche is Professor for Peace Research in Natural Sciences at PRIF and TU Darmstadt, co-speaker of the Cluster for Natural and Technical Science Arms Control Research (CNTR), and Head of the Research Group Science for Nuclear Diplomacy. He researches nuclear non-proliferation, arms control and disarmament with a focus on the development of verification methods and technologies.
