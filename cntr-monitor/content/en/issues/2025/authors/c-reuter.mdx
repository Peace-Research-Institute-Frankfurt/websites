Prof. Dr. Dr. Christian Reuter is Co-Head of the Research Group Emerging Disruptive Technologies and holds the chair of Science and Technology for Peace and Security (PEASEC) at TU Darmstadt. He works on the intersection of cyber security and privacy, peace and conflict studies as well as human-computer interaction. He and his team specifically contribute to peace informatics and technical peace research, crisis informatics, information warfare and usable safety, security and privacy.
