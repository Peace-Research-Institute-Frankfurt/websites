Dr. Yan-Jie Schnellbach heads the junior research group “nuSENTRY” (Neutrino-based Security Monitoring for Emerging Nuclear Technology and Reactor Types) at TU Darmstadt. His research focuses on nuclear non-proliferation and security monitoring, with an emphasis on detection and measurement technologies for nuclear facilities and future reactor technologies worldwide.
