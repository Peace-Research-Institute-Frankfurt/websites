Dr. Stefka Schmid is a research associate and post-doctoral researcher at the Chair of Science and Technology for Peace and Security (PEASEC) at TU Darmstadt. Her research interests include science and technology, in particular AI, as a subject of critical security studies as well as human-computer interaction in “safety-critical scenarios”.
