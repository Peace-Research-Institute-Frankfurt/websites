Fabian Unruh is a Doctoral Researcher in the Research Group Science for Nuclear Diplomacy. He works in the field of nuclear dis­armament verifi­cation and is interested in applying machine learning techniques in nuclear archaeo­logy for reconstruc­ting fissile material produc­tion histories.
