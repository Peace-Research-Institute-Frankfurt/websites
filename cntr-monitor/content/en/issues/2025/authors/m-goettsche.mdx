Prof. Dr. Malte Göttsche is Professor for Peace Research in Natural Sciences at PRIF and TU Darmstadt, Co-Speaker of CNTR, and Head of the Research Group Science for Nuclear Diplomacy. He researches nuclear nonproliferation, arms control and disarmament with a focus on the development of verification methods and technologies.
