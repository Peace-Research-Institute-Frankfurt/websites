Jean Pascal Zanders heads [The Trench](https://www.the-trench.org/), a research initiative dedicated to the future of disarmament. He was Leader of the Chemical and Biological Warfare Project at the Stockholm International Peace Research Institute (1996–2003), Director of the Geneva-based BioWeapons Prevention Project (2003–08), and Senior Research Fellow at the European Union Institute for Security Studies (2008–13). His research focusses on the long-term future of the BTWC and CWC and CBW disarmament in the Middle East. He currently chairs the OPCW Advisory Board on Education and Outreach (ABEO).
