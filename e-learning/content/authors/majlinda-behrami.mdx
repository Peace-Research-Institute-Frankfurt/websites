Majlinda Behrami is a researcher at the Peace Research Institute Frankfurt (PRIF), focusing on small arms control, gender, and the Women, Peace and Security agenda. She has held various roles in NGOs and think tanks across Europe, including the Flemish Peace Institute, where she worked on firearms-related gender-based violence in the EU.
