Linde Desmaele is an Assistant Professor of Intelligence and Security at the Institute of Security and Global Affairs (ISGA) at Leiden University. Her work is situated at the intersection of international relations and security studies, with a focus on nuclear politics, US alliances and European security. Before joining Leiden University, she held fellowships at MIT and Columbia University. She holds a PhD from the Vrije Universiteit Brussel, where her dissertation earned her the 2022 Global Strategy PhD Prize.
