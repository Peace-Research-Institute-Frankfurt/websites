### Quote

Both styles support a citation, an optional audio file and an optional link to the full document.

</Quote>

#### Style B (Document)

<Quote audio="eisenhower-chance-for-peace.mp3" type="document" fullDocument="https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/The_Chance_for_Peace" cite='Dwight D. Eisenhower, "Chance for Peace", speech to American Society of Newspaper Editors.'>

</Quote>
